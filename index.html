The VIZOR utilizes bleeding-edge human-machine interface (HMI) technology to simulate life-like realities using the brain's five senses. Not only that, the VIZOR allows its users to interact with one another in simulated spaces just as real as our own, over our very own VERSE Network.

EXPERIENCE NEW WORLDS


The VERSE Network allows anyone to explore randomly generated realities, whether they be as games, meeting places, or just to explore. The VERSE Network gives the power to the users: anything you can think of, you can make it.

MAKE FANTASY THE NEW REALITY


The VIZOR and VERSE Network are limitless in possibilities. Bring classic fantasy tales to life and experience them like never before, or create your own unique tales, stories, video games, adventures, and anything else you can think of with the power of the VERSE Network.
